  • DHAMAL

    • Summary;

    Dhamaal (2007) is a Hindi-language comedy film directed by Indra Kumar. The story follows four lazy, unemployed friends—Roy (Riteish Deshmukh), Adi (Arshad Warsi), Manav (Jaaved Jaaferi), and Boman (Aashish Chaudhary)—who stumble upon a dying gangster, Bose (Prem Chopra), who reveals the location of a hidden treasure worth ₹10 crore buried in Goa. Eager to find the fortune, the quartet embarks on a chaotic journey filled with comedic mishaps and obstacles. Their quest is further complicated by Inspector Kabir Nayak (Sanjay Dutt), who is also after the treasure. As the chase unfolds, the friends face various challenges, leading to a series of humorous situations. Ultimately, after numerous misadventures, the group finds themselves at a charity event for orphaned children. Moved by the children's plight, they decide to donate the treasure to the orphanage, realizing the value of friendship and generosity over wealth.   • ANABELLE

    • SUMMARY;

    Annabelle is a 2014 horror film centered around a possessed doll named Annabelle. The story follows a young couple, Mia and John Form, who experience terrifying supernatural events after John gives Mia a vintage doll for their baby. After a satanic cult attacks their home, the doll becomes a conduit for a demonic entity. As the evil presence grows stronger, the couple fights to protect their child and rid themselves of the cursed doll. The film is a prequel to The Conjuring and is part of The Conjuring Universe.
